Methodology Summary

  • Multi-Dimensional Feature Extraction: Specifically extracts pixel units from designated color channels (Red/Green/Blue) in multi-dimensional image arrays, accurately filtering out interference from non-target channels.
  • Calibration & Quantification: The Limit of Detection (LOD) can be automatically calculated based on NTC samples. The system incorporates a Four-Parameter Logistic (4PL) regression model to map the valid Mean Fluorescence Intensity (MFI) to absolute nucleic acid copy numbers.
