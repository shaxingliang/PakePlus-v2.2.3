Definition:

Standard Equation: Y = mX + b

Where:

  • Y: Ct (Threshold Cycle)
  • X: log10(DNA Copy Number)
  • m: Slope (typically between -3.1 and -3.6)
  • b: Intercept (related to PCR efficiency)

In real-time PCR, a lower Ct value indicates a higher initial amount of DNA template.

About Standard Curves

A qPCR standard curve is established by measuring known concentrations of DNA standards.

The equation follows the format: Y = mX + b, where X = log10(Copy Number).
